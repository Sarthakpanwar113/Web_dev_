Biography

linus Torvalds is a funusuh - amreica software engineer who is the creator and principle of the linux kernel, which is the kernal for number of operating system. He also created the version control After a visit to Transmeta in late 1996,[23] Torvalds accepted a position at the company in California, where he worked from February 1997 to June 2003.
